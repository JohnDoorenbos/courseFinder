Searching Tips

Department: Currently you can only search for a single department. Leaving it blank searches all departments. Departments should be searched by their abreviated forms (i.e. "BIO" not "biology"). This is not case sensitive. For a full list of abbreviations, see the catalog.

Keywords: This checks for keywords in the course title and official description. If you are having trouble getting results with this, try putting in related forms and related terms. For example, if "Rome" isn't returning many results, try also typing "Roman," "republic," and "empire". As a general rule, enter the singular version of a noun instead of the plural. If keywords is not blank, the results are sorted by relavence instead of alphabetically. Any course that matched at least one keyword is returned.

Gen eds: This limits the results by what general education requirement they fulfill. If this field is not blank, it returns only courses that fulfill at least one gen ed, sorted alphabetically. If you are having difficulty selecting more than one gen ed, try holding Ctrl and clicking.
